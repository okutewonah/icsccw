Abstract and Indexing

Following the peer review, all the accepted papers will be published in the Springer’s Series “Advances in Intelligent Systems and Computing”. This series are indexed by ISI Proceedings, EI-Compendex, DBLP, Web of Science, SCOPUS, Google Scholar and Springerlink – the highly recognized platforms.

Following the peer review and acceptance, the selected papers will be considered for possible publication in SCI journals:

  • Information Sciences Journal
  • The Soft Computing Journal
  • Artificial Intelligence in Medicine Journal
  • International Journal of Intelligent Automation and Soft Computing (AutoSoft)
