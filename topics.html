Scope and Objectives

The Conference on Theory and Applications Soft Computing, Computing with Words, Perception and Artificial Intelligence ICSCCW, is organized every other year with the aim of bringing together scientists, engineers and practitioners to exchange up-to-date knowledge and experience in the applications of Soft Computing and Computation With Words. The 11th ICSCCW-2021 will be held in Antalya, Turkey. The sessions will focus on the applications of Computing methods such as fuzzy logic, neural computing, evoltionary computing, machine learning, probabilistic reasoning and chaos theory in engineering, economy, business, finance, education; applications to other relevant areas are also welcome.

The sessions will focus on the applications of of Soft Computing methods such as fuzzy logic, neural computing, evoltionary computing, machine learning, probabilistic reasoning and chaos theory and hybrid systems in engineering, economy, business, finance, education; applications to other relevant areas are also welcome.

Topics of the Conference

Papers describing original work in, but not limited to, the following research areas are welcome:

  • Theory of Uncertain Computation
  • Theory and Application of Soft Computing
  • Decision Theory with Imperfect Information
  • Information Granulation
  • Fuzzy Logic and Neuro-Fuzzy Technology
  • Computing with Words and Perception
  • Probabilistic Reasoning
  • Intelligent Control
  • Computer Vision
  • Machine Learning, Deep Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Soft Computing and Artificial Intelligence-based Image Processing
  • Artificial Intelligence Tools
  • Soft Computing-based Simulations of Chaotic Process and Systems
  • Soft Computing and Artificial Intelligence in Engineering, Material Science, Business, Economics, Finance, Social Sciences, Medicine, Chemico-technological process, Pharmaceutical Sciences
  • Soft Computing and Internet of Things
  • Soft Computing and Industries 4 and 5
